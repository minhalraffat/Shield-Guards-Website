MOBILE PATROL

Our Mobile Officers are supported by our network of control centres and 24/7 operational managers...

read more

Shield guard utilises low emission vehicles and both the vehicle and officer(s) are tracked for both client and Shield guard monitoring purposes. Officers record activity on hand held devices. A mobile patrol works effectively for clients who feel a regular patrol or visit is a safe alternative to a static deployment.
